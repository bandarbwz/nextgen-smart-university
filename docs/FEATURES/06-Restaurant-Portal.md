# Restaurant Portal

## Overview

The Restaurant Portal allows restaurant owners to manage menus, orders, payments, and daily operations within the university Food Court.

Each restaurant has its own secure dashboard.

---

# Objectives

Restaurant owners can:

- Manage Restaurant Profile
- Manage Menu
- Receive Orders
- Update Order Status
- View Sales Reports
- View Customer Reviews

---

# Dashboard

Dashboard Widgets

- Today's Orders
- Pending Orders
- Preparing Orders
- Ready Orders
- Completed Orders
- Daily Revenue
- Average Rating

---

# Restaurant Profile

Owners can:

- Update Restaurant Information
- Upload Logo
- Update Opening Hours
- Update Contact Details

---

# Menu Management

Owners can:

- Add Menu Item
- Edit Menu Item
- Delete Menu Item
- Enable/Disable Menu Item
- Upload Images
- Organize Categories

---

# Order Management

Restaurant owners can:

- Accept Orders
- Reject Orders
- Mark Preparing
- Mark Ready
- Mark Completed

Students receive notifications after every status change.

---

# Payment

Restaurant owners can:

- View Payment History
- View Revenue
- Download Sales Reports

---

# Customer Reviews

Restaurant owners can:

- View Ratings
- View Reviews
- Reply to Reviews (Future Version)

---

# Reports

Generate:

- Daily Sales
- Weekly Sales
- Monthly Sales
- Best Selling Items
- Customer Ratings

Export Formats

- PDF
- Excel

---

# Notifications

Restaurant owners receive notifications for:

- New Order
- Cancelled Order
- Payment Completed
- Low Stock Alert

---

# Permissions

Restaurant owners can:

- Manage only their own restaurant.
- View only their own sales.

Cannot:

- Access other restaurants.
- Modify university settings.
- Access student academic data.
