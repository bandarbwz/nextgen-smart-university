# Food Court System

## Overview

The Food Court System provides a digital food ordering experience inside the university.

Students can browse restaurants, order meals, make payments, track orders, and rate restaurants without leaving the university platform.

Restaurant owners can manage menus, receive orders, and monitor daily business operations.

---

# Objectives

The Food Court System provides:

- Restaurant Directory
- Digital Menus
- Online Ordering
- Online Payment
- Order Tracking
- Customer Reviews
- Sales Reports

---

# Student Features

Students can:

- Browse Restaurants
- View Menus
- Search Food
- Add Items to Cart
- Place Orders
- Pay Online
- Track Orders
- View Order History
- Save Favorite Restaurants
- Rate Restaurants

---

# Restaurant Features

Restaurant owners can:

- Manage Restaurant Profile
- Manage Categories
- Add Menu Items
- Update Prices
- Upload Food Images
- Accept Orders
- Reject Orders
- Update Order Status

---

# Order Workflow

Student selects restaurant.

↓

Student adds food to cart.

↓

Student confirms order.

↓

Payment is completed.

↓

Restaurant receives order.

↓

Restaurant prepares order.

↓

Student receives notification.

↓

Student collects order.
