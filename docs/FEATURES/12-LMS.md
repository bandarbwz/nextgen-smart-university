# Learning Management System (LMS)

## Overview

The Learning Management System (LMS) provides all learning resources and academic content in one centralized platform.

It replaces external learning systems by allowing lecturers and students to manage teaching and learning activities directly inside the university portal.

---

# Objectives

The LMS provides:

- Learning Materials
- Assignments
- Quizzes
- Online Exams
- Grades
- Announcements
- Course Resources

---

# Lecturer Features

Lecturers can:

- Upload Materials
- Upload Videos
- Create Assignments
- Create Quizzes
- Publish Announcements
- Grade Students
- Give Feedback

---

# Student Features

Students can:

- Download Materials
- Watch Videos
- Submit Assignments
- Take Quizzes
- View Grades
- Read Announcements

---

# Assignment Workflow

Lecturer creates assignment.

↓

Students receive notification.

↓

Students submit assignment.

↓

Lecturer grades submission.

↓

Students receive grades.

---

# Quiz Workflow

Lecturer creates quiz.

↓

Students complete quiz.

↓

System grades automatically (where applicable).

↓

Results are published.

---
